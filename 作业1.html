Key lime pie

A key lime pie is a North American dessert pie.It's a bit like a lemon meringue pie but made with Key limes from the Florida Keys area.Here's a photo of one

Photo credit:Averette.The liccence is CC-BY-SA 3.0

Ingredients

Common ingredients include:
  1. Eggs
  2. Key Limes
  3. Graham crackers(for the crust)
If you want to buy Graham crackers in Auckland, you need to go to Martha's Backyard in Mt Wellington,which sells many products impported from the USA
